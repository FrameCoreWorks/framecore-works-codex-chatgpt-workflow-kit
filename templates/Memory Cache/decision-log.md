# Decision Log

## Purpose

Record durable decisions that affect workflow continuation.

| Time UTC | Decision | Evidence | Owner | Follow-Up |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| replace-me | Initial Memory Cache created. | user request or setup command | user | keep project-state.md current |

## Rules

- Record decisions, not full chat history.
- Keep entries short and recoverable.
- Do not include secrets, private URLs, raw transcripts, or chain-of-thought.
