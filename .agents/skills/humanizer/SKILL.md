# Humanizer

Make text sound specific, human, and useful.

Preserve:

- factual claims
- exact required copy
- legal or compliance wording
- product names and approved terminology
- user-approved tone constraints

Improve:

- rhythm
- clarity
- concrete language
- sentence variety
- transitions
- audience fit

Do not add unsupported claims.
